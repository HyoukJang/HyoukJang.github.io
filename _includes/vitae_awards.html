대한민국 인재상 Talent Award of KOREA

Dec. 2017

The Talent Award of Korea (Korean: 대한민국 인재상) is an award bestowed by the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Education of Korea from 2014. Until 2013, this was the Talent Medal of Korea, bestowed by the President of the Republic of Korea with a post-nominal (KTM) and Presidential Medal. It recognizes those individuals who are likely to become Korea's future leaders and have performed exemplary talents or outstanding meritorious service. 50 high school students, 40 college students, and 10 adults are selected based on their intelligence, passion, creativity, and community spirit.
